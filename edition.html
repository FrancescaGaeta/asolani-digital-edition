Text selection

For each version, the text is transcribed from the incipit of Book I up to the second poetic section. The dedicatory letter in the 1505 edition is treated as a separate paratextual section and is not included in the parallel comparison.

Parallel comparison

The three versions (Q, 1505, 1553) are presented as autonomous texts. They are aligned by macro-textual units (Prose 1 → Verses 1 → Prose 2 → Verses 2) in order to enable a parallel comparison without conflating the textual traditions.

Critical apparatus

A selective negative critical apparatus is used to document significant textual variants between the versions, without attempting to reconstruct a single archetypal text.
