Project aim

This project presents a sample digital scholarly edition based on the critical edition of Gli Asolani edited by Dilemmi. The selected texts (Q, 1505, 1553) are treated as autonomous versions and compared in parallel.

Texts and witnesses

  • Q (Querini Stampalia): autograph witness, fundamental for Book I.
  • 1505: early printed edition including an initial dedicatory letter.
  • 1553: later redaction (text 16), representing a revised version of the work.
